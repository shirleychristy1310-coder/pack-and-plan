Palolem Beach, Goa

Palolem Beach, located in South Goa, is a serene paradise known for its crescent-shaped coastline lined with swaying palm trees and calm turquoise waters. It's one of Goa's most picturesque beaches, offering the perfect mix of relaxation and gentle adventure.

Things to Do

Palolem offers everything from lazy beach lounging to kayaking across its peaceful bay. You can explore Butterfly Island by boat, take yoga classes at seaside retreats, or enjoy live music at the famous beach shacks. During evenings, the Silent Noise Party - a headphone party under the stars - is a must experience event unique to Palolem.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Palolem Beach is between November and March, when the weather is sunny yet breezy. The sea remains calm and ideal for swimming, and most cafes and resorts are open during this season, giving the beach its lively charm.
