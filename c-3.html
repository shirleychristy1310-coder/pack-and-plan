Chennai - The Gateway to South India

Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu, is a blend of traditional culture and modern growth. It's known for its ancient temples, classical music, delicious cuisine, and coastal charm. The citys warmth and hospitality make every visitor feel at home.

Things to Do

Explore the ancient Kapaleeshwarar Temple in Mylapore, admire colonial architecture at Fort St. George, and visit the Government Museum for a glimpse of history. Relax at Marina Beach or shop for silk sarees at T. Nagar. The city's vibrant food culture, from filter coffee to street snacks, is a delight for food lovers.

Best Time to Visit

The most comfortable time to visit Chennai is between November and February when the weather is cool and breezy. The city comes alive with the Margazhi music festival, offering classical dance and music performances across various venues.
