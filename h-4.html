Ellora Caves , Maharashtra

The Ellora Caves, carved between the 6th and 10th centuries, are a marvel of unity — Hindu, Jain, and Buddhist temples existing side by side. The Kailasa Temple, carved from a single rock, is one of the world’s grandest monolithic creations.

Things to Do

Explore Cave 16 (Kailasa Temple), wander through the Buddhist caves, and marvel at Jain art’s intricate detail.

Best Time to Visit

October to February, when the climate is cool and comfortable.
