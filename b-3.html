Kovalam Beach, Kerala

Kovalam Beach, located near Thiruvananthapuram, is one of the most famous beach destinations in Kerala. Known for its crescent-shaped coastline, calm waves, and serene ambience, Kovalam offers the perfect blend of relaxation and natural beauty. The tall coconut trees and golden sands make it a paradise for travelers seeking peace and adventure alike.

Things to Do

At Kovalam, you can enjoy sunbathing, swimming, and surfing. The lighthouse beach is popular for panoramic views from the top of the Vizhinjam Lighthouse. Visitors can also indulge in Ayurvedic massages, take catamaran rides, or explore the local handicraft shops along the shore. The nearby Hawah Beach and Samudra Beach add more charm to your visit.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kovalam Beach is from November to March, when the weather is cool and breezy. During this season, the sea remains calm, making it ideal for water sports and seaside relaxation. Monsoons bring a different kind of beauty, perfect for nature enthusiasts who love the sight of rain over the Arabian Sea.
