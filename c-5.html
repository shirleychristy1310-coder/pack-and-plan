Bengaluru, Karnataka

Bengaluru, also known as the Garden City of India, is a vibrant blend of modern innovation and traditional charm. With its pleasant weather, lush parks, and lively nightlife, the city stands as a hub for technology, culture, and cuisine.

Things to Do

Start your day at Cubbon Park or Lalbagh Botanical Garden, both perfect for morning strolls and photography. Explore the historical Bangalore Palace and Vidhana Soudha to witness the city's regal and administrative grandeur. For shopping and food, visit MG Road, Brigade Road, or Indiranagar. Don't miss the buzzing cafes and street food scenes that make Bengaluru a food lover's delight.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bengaluru is from October to February when the weather is cool and comfortable. Evenings during these months are perfect for outdoor exploration and enjoying rooftop dining experiences that overlook the city lights.
