Kangchenjunga Mountain, Sikkim

The majestic Kangchenjunga, the world’s third-highest peak (8,586 m), stands as a shimmering crown of Sikkim, wrapped in mist and legend. Revered by locals as a guardian deity, its snow-clad beauty captivates every heart that beholds it.

Things to Do

Trek through Yuksom to Goecha La, visit the Khecheopalri Lake, explore Kanchenjunga National Park, and watch sunrise from Tshoka or Pelling.

Best Time to Visit

March to May and September to November, when skies are clear and rhododendrons paint the trails in color.
