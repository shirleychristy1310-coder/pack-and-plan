Varkala Beach, Kerala

Varkala Beach, also known as Papanasam Beach, is a serene coastal haven located along the Arabian Sea in Kerala. Famous for its stunning cliffs, natural springs, and calm atmosphere, it offers the perfect blend of beauty, spirituality, and adventure.

Things to Do

Varkala is a paradise for beach lovers and adventure seekers alike. You can relax on the sandy shores, enjoy paragliding from the red cliffs, or explore the vibrant beachside cafes that serve authentic Kerala cuisine. Visit the Janardhana Swamy Temple nearby and take a dip in the natural mineral springs believed to have healing properties.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal months to visit Varkala Beach are from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and sightseeing. The evenings offer mesmerizing sunset views from the cliffside cafes and walking trails.
