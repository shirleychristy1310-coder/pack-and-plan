Delhi - The Heart of India

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant blend of history, culture, and modernity. From majestic Mughal architecture to bustling markets and contemporary cafes, Delhi offers every traveler a unique and colorful experience. The city beautifully bridges the past and the present through its ancient monuments and urban lifestyle.

Things to Do

Start your journey with the Red Fort and Qutub Minar, two UNESCO World Heritage sites showcasing India's grand Mughal past. Visit India Gate and the Rashtrapati Bhavan for iconic landmarks. Wander through Chandni Chowk for street food like chole bhature, parathas, and jalebis. Don't miss Connaught Place for shopping and cafes or Lotus Temple for a serene experience.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Delhi is between October and March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing. The winter season brings festive vibes with Diwali lights, cultural fairs, and outdoor dining experiences all across the city.
