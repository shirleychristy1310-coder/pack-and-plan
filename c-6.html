Hyderabad, Telangana

Hyderabad, known as the City of Pearls, is a vibrant blend of history, culture, and modern charm. With its grand palaces, bustling bazaars, and mouthwatering biryanis, Hyderabad stands as one of India's most iconic travel destinations.

Things to Do

Start your journey at the magnificent Charminar, the citys symbol of pride. Explore the royal Chowmahalla Palace, and spend your evening at the picturesque Hussain Sagar Lake. Don't miss shopping at Laad Bazaar for pearls and bangles, and indulge in authentic Hyderabadi Biryani at Paradise or Bawarchi.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Hyderabad is between October and February when the weather is pleasant. Winter is ideal for exploring historic monuments and outdoor attractions without the scorching heat.
