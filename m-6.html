Doddabetta,Tamil Nadu

At 2,637 m, Doddabetta is the crown of the Nilgiri Hills, where misty winds meet tea-scented air. Just 9 km from Ooty, it offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats — green, glowing, and gentle.

Things to Do

Visit the Doddabetta Viewpoint, explore Ooty Tea Factory, trek through Botanical Gardens, and enjoy a cup of hot chai overlooking the clouds.

Best Time to Visit

October to May, when the weather is cool and the skies are clear.
