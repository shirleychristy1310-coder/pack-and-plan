Hampi , Karnataka

Once the glorious capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi now lies in poetic ruins beside the Tungabhadra River. Boulders, temples, and palaces whisper tales of grandeur long gone but never forgotten.

Things to Do

Climb Matanga Hill for sunrise, visit Virupaksha Temple, explore Vittala Temple, and cycle through the ancient bazaars.

Best Time to Visit

November to February, when the sun softens and the stones stay cool.
