Mumbai, Maharashtra

Mumbai, the city of dreams, is India's financial and entertainment capital. Known for its vibrant energy, colonial architecture, bustling streets, and Arabian Sea coastline, Mumbai is a city that never sleeps and always welcomes you with open arms.

Things to Do

Visit the Gateway of India and take a ferry to Elephanta Caves. Enjoy the sunset at Marine Drive or relax on Juhu Beach. Explore the heritage of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus and shop at Colaba Causeway. Don't miss tasting Mumbai's famous street food like vada pav, pav bhaji, and bhel puri.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mumbai is between November and February when the weather is cool and comfortable. These months are ideal for sightseeing and exploring outdoor attractions without the discomfort of heat or monsoon rains.
